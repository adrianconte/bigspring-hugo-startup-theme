OUR APPROACH

EC COASTAL delivers expert advice within the industrial port and energy sectors and for the sustainable development of our littorals.

We pride ourselves in developing comprehensive and practical solutions to complex coastal problems and believe that a continuous community based approach is key in order to construct a durable response to manage changes in the coastal zone.

Our foundation is a precise knowledge of the capabilities and restrictions of physical and numerical modelling tools and first hand experience of a wide range of marine environments. With our flexible client focused attitude and unique perspective, we bring reliability, quality and value to any coastal project.

OVERVIEW OF SERVICES

Our services are:

  • Coastal protection designs
  • Marina and breakwater layouts
  • Integrated coastal management plans
  • Sediment transport assessments
  • Physical and numerical modelling assistance

EC COASTAL can team with other disciplines such as architects, geotechnical and structural engineers, hydraulic laboratories and surveyors.

The OWNER

The company is owned and operated by Sylvain Perrin, a professional engineer who has provided consultancy services in coastal and port development. As an engineer based either in France, Australia or the UAE, he has built a reputation in delivering successful projects that exceed client expectations. With a methodical approach and a commitment to quality and excellence, Sylvain led major coastal management plans in France and Africa. His strengths include the design of protective marine structures and the application of a wide range of models to test their performance.
